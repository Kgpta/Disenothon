Wearables

Wearables have come into their own over the last year with 2015 seeing some great products announced from Tag Heuer and Apple's smartwatches to Fitbit and Jawbone's activity trackers. If you thought this area of tech was just a fad, you better think again because if CES 2016 is anything to go by, it's only going to get more exciting. As always, the consumer electronics show in Vegas marks the announcement of thousands of products from huge TVs to smaller, whacky gadgets. This feature focuses on the best smartwatches and wearables that have been launched at the show.
